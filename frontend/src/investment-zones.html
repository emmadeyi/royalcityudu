ROYAL RESORT AND TOURIST CENTER

...getting fun, leisure and relaxation out of life

The Royal Resort and Tourist Center in Udu, is an independent project aimed at promoting family holiday, general entertainment, business meetings, and Tourism in Delta State of Nigeria.
It is intended to be a multi-purpose Resort, taking care of Water sports, Gulf sport, Horse racing Sports, Field and track sports (Football and Athletics), Family amusement and fun, Cultural promotion and exchange, Nollywood presence, Social events and ceremonies, Religious events, Professional entertainment, Peaceful leisure and rest.

WELCOME TO THE ROYAL CITY INDUSTRIAL WORLD

...making Nigeria and Africa more productive

The industrial plan in the Royal City is to attract and develop fifty (50) light industries of medium scale; that will collectively produce essential consumer goods for domestic consumption and export purposes, mostly to the rest of Africa.

The Industrial agenda, is designed to magnet domestic and foreign investments, to enable factory development, technology transfer, job creation, capacity building, growth of personal and corporate savings; and to generate more foreign exchange earnings, for the host country Nigeria, while improving the standard of living in Africa at large.

COMMERCIAL ZONE

...PROMOTING INTRA-AFRICA AND GLOBAL TRADE

The Commercial Zone in the Royal City is designed for 24 hour safe trading activities. It will accommodate five hundred (500) residential warehouses; five hundred (500) pure storage warehouses and five hundred (500) Micro- warehouses.
The commercial plan is to pro-actively create the most modern, most robust and high bustling international trade area in Nigeria, Africa... where imports, exports, wholesale trade, exchange of goods and services with other African countries, becomes the mainstay.

AUTO GALLERIA



The Auto-Galleria in the Royal City is specially created as a major center for trade in different categories of automobiles, ranging from small cars, clean jeeps and buses; to large luxurious buses, strong haulage trucks and industrial earth- moving tractors.
The reason for having an Auto-Galleria in the Royal City is, to expand the sources of good and cheap supplies of new and used vehicles in the Nigeria market, besides (Lagos, Nigeria and Cotonou, Benin Republic).
